Deloitte Excel Workbench Validator
=================================

QUICK START:
1. Double-click DeloitteExcelValidator (or .exe on Windows)
2. Wait for the terminal to show "Running on http://localhost:5001"
3. Open your browser to http://localhost:5001
4. Upload your workbook and rules files
5. Download your validation report

REQUIREMENTS:
- No additional software needed
- Files must be .xlsx or .xlsm format

SUPPORT:
- Close the terminal window to stop the application
- If browser doesn't open automatically, go to http://localhost:5001
